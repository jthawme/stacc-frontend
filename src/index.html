Stacc is an application to convert video files into high quality Gifs. It uses FFMPEG with complex filters to make more aware decisions about your conversion, meaning the filesize can stay down, but the quality can still look like the input video.

Privacy

All technology is used strictly on your own machine. Therefore it works entirely offline, just as it does online, but more importantly that means it never connects, collects or corrects (?) any of your personal data in any way. Anonymous connection is made to check for updates, but never transfers any info about you or your machine.
